# Audit Scope — Accessibility and Keyboard Navigation

## Critical Screens to Audit

The audit focuses on the core user journey and administrative tasks to ensure the platform is usable by all team members, including those relying on keyboard navigation or assistive technologies.

1.  **Login Screen:** Primary entry point.
2.  **Project Dashboard:** Main overview and entry to work items.
3.  **Worklist:** The primary workbench for finding and selecting indicators.
4.  **Recurring Queue:** Operational area for submitting and approving recurring evidence.
5.  **Indicator Detail + AI Assist Panel:** Deep work area including evidence management and AI-guided assistance.
6.  **Settings Page:** Navigation hub for administrative tasks.
7.  **Admin Masters:** Critical management of controlled vocabularies.

## Audit Criteria

- **Keyboard Usability:** Logical TAB order, focus visibility, modal trapping, and activation of all controls via ENTER/SPACE.
- **Labeling:** Presence and clarity of labels for all inputs and descriptive text for buttons.
- **Semantics:** Proper use of HTML elements (headings, buttons, links) to ensure predictability.
- **Feedback:** Clear visibility of error states and focus rings.
