## Route-level notes

- `IndicatorDrawer` is the primary worklist interaction surface from both `ProjectWorklistScreen` and `ProjectWorkspaceBoard`; neither surface links into `/project-indicators/:id`.
- The full indicator detail page is mounted and working, but it is not discoverable from sidebar/topbar or from the primary worklist cards.
- Admin routes are additionally guarded in the page layer via `AdminAreaGuard`, while all workbench routes are protected by middleware plus `AuthGuard`.
