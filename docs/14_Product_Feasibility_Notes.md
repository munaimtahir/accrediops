# Product feasibility notes

## What is clearly achievable now

- internal checklist/workspace management
- indicator categorization
- evidence tracking
- recurring evidence monitoring
- Excel/CSV exports
- print-pack preparation
- reuse/cloning for new clients
- manual AI drafting and assessment support

## What is achievable but should be controlled carefully

- reusable document generation with variable replacement
- stronger evidence intelligence across clients
- AI-assisted classification and assessment

## What should not be treated as a first-step requirement

- fully autonomous AI processing
- client self-service
- public-facing product packaging
- heavy workflow automation before the model is stable

## Strategic note

Manual AI integration is the correct first stance for this product because:
- it reduces technical overhead
- it matches your preference
- it keeps human accountability intact
- it lowers drift risk
